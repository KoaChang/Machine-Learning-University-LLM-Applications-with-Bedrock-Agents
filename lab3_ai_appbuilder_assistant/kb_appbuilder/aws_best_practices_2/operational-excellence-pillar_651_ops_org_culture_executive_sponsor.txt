Senior leadership is the sponsor, advocate, and\n    driver for the adoption of best practices and evolution of the\n    organization\n  \nBenefits of establishing this best\n    practice: Engaged leadership, clearly communicated\n    expectations, and shared goals ensures that team members know what\n    is expected of them. Evaluating success aids in identification of\n    barriers to success so that they can be addressed through\n    intervention by the sponsor advocate or their delegates.\n  \nLevel of risk exposed if this best practice\n    is not established: High\n  \nImplementation guidance\n\n\n\n          Executive Sponsorship: Senior leadership clearly sets\n          expectations for the organization and evaluates success.\n          Senior leadership is the sponsor, advocate, and driver for the\n          adoption of best practices and evolution of the organization\n        \n\n\n\n              Set expectations: Define and publish goals for your\n              organizations including how they will be measured.\n            \n\n\n              Track achievement of goals: Measure the incremental\n              achievement of goals regularly and share the results so\n              that appropriate action can be taken if outcomes are at\n              risk.\n            \n\n\n              Provide the resources necessary to achieve your goals:\n              Regularly review if resources are still appropriate, of if\n              additional resources are needed based on: new information,\n              changes to goals, responsibilities, or your business\n              environment.\n            \n\n\n              Advocate for your teams: Remain engaged with your teams so\n              that you understand how they are doing and if there are\n              external factors affecting them. When your teams are\n              impacted by external factors, reevaluate goals and adjust\n              targets as appropriate. Identify obstacles that are\n              impeding your teams progress. Act on behalf of your teams\n              to help address obstacles and remove unnecessary burdens.\n            \n\n\n              Be a driver for adoption of best practices: Acknowledge\n              best practices that provide quantifiable benefits and\n              recognize the creators and adopters. Encourage further\n              adoption to magnify the benefits achieved.\n            \n\n\n              Be a driver for evolution of for your teams: Create a\n              culture of continual improvement. Encourage both personal\n              and organizational growth and development. Provide long\n              term targets to strive for that will require incremental\n              achievement over time. Adjust this vision to compliment\n              your needs, business goals, and business environment as\n              they change.\n            \n\n\n Javascript is disabled or is unavailable in your browser.To use the Amazon Web Services Documentation, Javascript must be enabled.
